# Primitive Shield

[![Hackathon Winner](https://img.shields.io/badge/YCI_Hackathon_2026-1st_Place-gold)](https://github.com/atakanturg/primitive-shield)
[![Deployment Status](https://img.shields.io/website?url=https%3A%2F%2Fshield.primitive-os.cc)](https://shield.primitive-os.cc)

Primitive Shield is a rapid-response civic-tech platform engineered to combat predatory evictions and mitigate the displacement of vulnerable residents during the gentrification process in the Miami area. 

Developed under a strict 24-hour development cycle at the **YCI Hackathon Challenge (May 17, 2026)** in Miami, FL, this project was awarded **First Place** for its community impact, engineering execution, and innovative use of AI.

## Problem & Solution

### The Challenge
Predatory landlords frequently exploit complex legal jargon and aggressive eviction notices to displace tenants who lack immediate access to legal counsel, accelerating predatory gentrification cycles.

### The Solution
Primitive Shield leverages deterministic prompt engineering workflows to digest complex, localized eviction threats and generate structured, context-aware mitigation strategies for affected tenants. It democratizes access to tenant-rights education by translating adversarial legal threats into clear, actionable steps.
